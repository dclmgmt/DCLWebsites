ABOUT US

Bundu Khan provides a great environment to eat on-the-go, relax and
dine in, upgrade a typical office meeting or host that special event.

We offer our customers a sophisticated blend of modern surroundings,
terrific atmosphere and a friendly efficient service, combined with a
menu that offers an outstanding choice of dishes and Bundu Khan
specialties (not available any where else in the UK) such as the ’
Bihari Boti kebab’ with ‘our very own tamarind sauce’, not least our
non-alcoholic cocktail bar serving the finest drinks.

BUNDU FLAVOUR

The Bundu Khan specialties, the paratha, kebab and bihari boti kebab are prepared from ‘secret’ recipes passed down for generations.

Our menu is inspired and created by our head chef Mr Khan who has the accolade of serving the last four prime ministers and politicians whilst at Horseferry road.

Bundu khan carefully chooses high quality, natural ingredients for its gourmet offerings with daily trips to selected suppliers. Our lunch & dinner menus range well beyond traditional Indian/Pakistani cuisine into specialties and ethnic dishes.

BUNDU MAOCKTAILS
Wherever cocktails came from they are here to stay and keep developing at phenomenal speed-from the classical art deco, black-tie images of the 20’s and 30’s to the umbrella-garnished monsters of the 1960’s and 1970’s.

Our non-alcoholic cocktail bar aims to inspire a drinking culture based on the use of quality ingredients, great flavours and where the craft of the professional bartender is recognised and appreciated.



PRIVATE PARTIES

Bundu Khan offers delectable authentic cuisine to private events

Our spacious banqueting hall with its own sound system and air-conditioning is capable of catering for up to 120 guests, be it a corporate banquet or conference (wireless internet available), a wedding, an engagement party, a birthday or any special occasion

Our premium and specially crafted menus are ideal for any event.
